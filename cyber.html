ABOUT CYBER SECURITY

What is cyber security

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting internet-connected systems, including hardware, software, and data, from theft, damage, or unauthorized access. It involves implementing measures to prevent cyber attacks, detecting and responding to security incidents, and maintaining the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information.

Why is cyber security important

Cybersecurity is important because it helps protect against unauthorized access, theft, and damage to sensitive data and systems. With the increasing use of technology in our daily lives, cyber attacks are becoming more sophisticated and frequent. A successful cyber attack can result in financial loss, reputation damage, and even legal consequences. Implementing strong cybersecurity measures can help prevent these incidents and ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information
