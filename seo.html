What is SEO

  • SEO stands for search engine optimization, which is a set of practices designed to improve the appearance and positioning of web pages in organic search results.
    Because organic search is the most prominent way for people to discover and access online content, a good SEO strategy is essential for improving the quality and quantity of traffic to your website.

Why is SEO important

  • SEO is important because organic search is the most prominent way for people to discover and access online content. A good SEO strategy is essential for improving the quality and quantity of traffic to your website.
    By optimizing your website for search engines, you can increase your visibility and attract more potential customers to your site. This can ultimately lead to increased sales and revenue for your business.
